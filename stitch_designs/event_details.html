About the Event

Join us for an evening of insightful discussion and networking as we explore how digital transformation is redefining leadership roles across various industries. This flagship event brings together distinguished alumni, industry pioneers, and academic experts for a deep dive into the future of work, ethical AI, and strategic decision-making in a hyper-connected world.

Whether you're a seasoned executive or a rising professional, this summit offers a unique platform to exchange ideas, share challenges, and build lasting connections within the Alumni Connect community. Expect high-energy panels followed by a curated networking reception.
